# Site Type Classification Decision-Making Checklist

## Instructions
Answer each question with Yes or No. Count the number of "Yes" answers in each section. The section with more "Yes" answers indicates your recommended site type.

## Industrial Site Indicators
- [ ] Is the site primarily focused on production, generation, or processing of goods/energy?
- [ ] Does the site operate 24/7 with continuous physical processes?
- [ ] Does the site have a high concentration of OT systems (SCADA, DCS, PLCs)?
- [ ] Does the site require specialized physical and cyber protection for critical infrastructure?
- [ ] Is the site subject to industrial regulations (NERC CIP, IEC 62443)?
- [ ] Does the site house equipment that directly controls physical processes?
- [ ] Is the site located near resource inputs or distribution networks?
- [ ] Does the site require specialized environmental controls for industrial processes?
- [ ] Are operations staff the primary personnel at this location?
- [ ] Would site disruption directly impact physical production or energy generation?

## Enterprise Site Indicators
- [ ] Is the site focused on core business operations and corporate management?
- [ ] Does the site house executive leadership and strategic decision-makers?
- [ ] Does the site have a mix of IT and OT systems with enterprise-grade infrastructure?
- [ ] Does the site require comprehensive security with multiple defense layers?
- [ ] Is the site subject to multiple compliance requirements (SOX, ISO 27001)?
- [ ] Does the site serve as a central hub for business operations across multiple locations?
- [ ] Does the site house critical business data and intellectual property?
- [ ] Does the site have advanced communications infrastructure?
- [ ] Are business leaders and knowledge workers the primary personnel?
- [ ] Would site disruption impact strategic business functions across the organization?

## Corporate Site Indicators
- [ ] Is the site focused on business administration and departmental operations?
- [ ] Does the site operate primarily during standard business hours?
- [ ] Does the site have primarily IT systems with limited OT connectivity?
- [ ] Does the site implement standard enterprise security controls?
- [ ] Is the site subject to standard business compliance requirements?
- [ ] Does the site serve regional or departmental functions?
- [ ] Does the site house mid-level management and administrative staff?
- [ ] Is the site in a commercial office setting?
- [ ] Would site disruption impact specific business functions but not critical operations?
- [ ] Can most site functions be temporarily relocated if needed?

## Branch Site Indicators
- [ ] Is the site focused on local operations, customer service, or field support?
- [ ] Does the site have a limited operational scope with standard hours?
- [ ] Does the site have basic IT infrastructure with centralized management?
- [ ] Does the site implement standardized security with central management?
- [ ] Is the site subject to minimal local compliance requirements?
- [ ] Does the site primarily serve local customers or support local operations?
- [ ] Does the site have a small staff footprint?
- [ ] Can the site function with minimal local IT support?
- [ ] Would site disruption have limited impact on overall business operations?
- [ ] Is the site in a retail, service center, or small office setting?

**Branch Site Score:** _____ / 10

## Recommendation
- Highest score indicates the recommended site type classification
- If scores are tied, consider the site's primary purpose and operational impact as tiebreakers
- For mixed-use sites, consider classifying different areas separately
